Pomodoro is a time management and endurance technique. Work is broken down into
25 minute intervals, followed by a short five minute break. After 4 Pomodoros,
the break time is expanded to ten minutes. This project was an exercise in using
the pomodoro technique in pair programming. It was challenging to work with
someone that I'd never worked with before, but it was a valuable exercise in
communication and patience.
